St Beuno's Flavoured Milk Rebrand

St Beuno's is a Welsh dairy brand that produces a range of flavoured milks. I performed a rebrand for this line when they wanted to a modern, updated image that was fun but gave a premium feel. It is particularly targeted at young people so bright colours and dramatic illustrations were selected. The final products did well achieve the final brief and gave the brand a much needed freshness.
