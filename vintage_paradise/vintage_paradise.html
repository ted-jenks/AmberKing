Resume
In Summer 2021, I was hired as a content creator for Vintage Paradise, a vintage store based on Brick Lane, London. In my time with them, I focussed primarily on TikTok where I created videos based around current trends and popular sounds to get the brand's name out there and show off the clothes. Over the 4 months I worked with them, we saw fantastic results, with the number of TikTok followers more than doubling and the number of Instagram followers rising by 62%.
